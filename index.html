the azouzia of sidi bou said

Sidi Bou Said, the mystical hill, where Raïs El Abhar rests surrounded by 66 holy presences, from Sidi Dhrif, to Sidi Chabaane, and from Sidi Faoueh ll misk to Sidi Azizi. The mystical hill where each street spreading a blue and white mystery opens up to welcome for six days from Wednesday August 23 the Rouhaniyet festival.

Rouhanyet is a spiritual walk in the garden of the mystical music of the world, beyond beliefs and religions, a message of love, peace, and brotherhood. The lighthouse will illuminate with its lights the infinite love songs of Jalel-Eddine El-Roumi, Ibn Arabi, Shams of Tabriz, El Hallej, Rabàa El Adawiya...a universal and solar song, 150 hours non stop with 20 shows, workshops, yoga and many other events. Rouhaniyet brings together the five continents for a moment of pure mystical communion, gnawas, gospel, soulamya and Sufi songs, stambeli, and qawali...

Rouhanyet is the result of ROSTOM FOUNDATION which is a non-profit association, which intervenes in the social, educational and cultural field. They implement actions that concern all areas of social and cultural life in order to develop positive energies, and new mentalities based on personal development, individual fulfillment, harmony, self-confidence and self-esteem. universal love.
